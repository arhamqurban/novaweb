Pakistan's e-commerce market is growing exponentially. With increasing internet penetration and digital payment adoption, now is the time to build or upgrade your online store.

## 1. Mobile-First Shopping Experience

Over 70% of e-commerce traffic in Pakistan comes from mobile devices. Your store must be designed for small screens first, with:
- Large, tappable buttons
- Simplified checkout flows
- Fast loading on 3G/4G connections
- Touch-friendly navigation

## 2. Multiple Payment Options

Pakistan's payment landscape is diverse. Successful e-commerce stores support:
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Local payment gateways
- Cash on delivery (still the most popular option)
- Digital wallets (JazzCash, Easypaisa)
- Buy now, pay later options

## 3. Trust-Building Features

Pakistani consumers are understandably cautious about online shopping. Build trust with:
- Clear return and refund policies
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Secure payment badges
- Real-time order tracking
- WhatsApp customer support

## 4. Fast Loading Speeds

Every second of delay costs you conversions. For Pakistani audiences with varying internet speeds, optimization is crucial:
- Compress images without sacrificing quality
- Use a content delivery network (CDN)
- Minimize JavaScript and CSS
- Implement lazy loading

## 5. Localized Content

Speak your customers' language — both literally and figuratively:
- Urdu language support for key pages
- Local currency (PKR) with proper formatting
- Culturally appropriate imagery
- Local shipping information and timelines
