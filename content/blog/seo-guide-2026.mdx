Search Engine Optimization (SEO) remains the most cost-effective way to attract qualified leads to your website. For Lahore-based businesses, local SEO is particularly powerful.

## Why Local SEO Matters for Lahore Businesses

When someone searches for "web design agency in Lahore", Google shows local results first. If your business isn't optimized for local search, you're invisible to the customers who are actively looking for your services.

### Key Local SEO Factors:

1. **Google Business Profile** — Ensure your profile is verified, complete, and regularly updated
2. **Local Keywords** — Include Lahore and Pakistan in your key search terms
3. **Local Citations** — Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all directories
4. **Local Backlinks** — Links from other Lahore businesses and organizations
5. **Reviews** — Positive Google reviews significantly impact local rankings

## Technical SEO Foundations

### Page Speed
Google uses Core Web Vitals as ranking signals. Focus on:
- Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) < 2.5 seconds
- First Input Delay (FID) < 100 milliseconds
- Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) < 0.1

### Mobile Optimization
With over 70% of searches in Pakistan happening on mobile devices, mobile-first indexing is critical. Your site must perform flawlessly on smartphones.

### Structured Data
Implement schema markup to help Google understand your content. LocalBusiness schema, FAQ schema, and Article schema can dramatically improve how your site appears in search results.

## Common SEO Mistakes to Avoid

- **Keyword stuffing** — Modern SEO prioritizes topic relevance over keyword density
- **Ignoring meta descriptions** — These are your ad copy in search results
- **Broken links** — Regularly audit your site for 404 errors
- **Duplicate content** — Each page should have unique, valuable content

## Measuring Success

Track these metrics monthly:
- Organic traffic growth
- Keyword rankings for target terms
- Click-through rates from search results
- Conversion rates from organic traffic
- Google Business Profile insights
